Problem Statement

Modern universities face critical information bottlenecks that hinder administrative efficiency:

Process Latency

Manual verification of fee slips and roll numbers causes student frustration and office overcrowding.

Static Access

University data is often trapped in static DOCX files or isolated SQL tables, inaccessible to 24/7 inquiries.

Inaccurate Feedback

Traditional bots often hallucinate or provide outdated campus information without live verification.

Aim & Objectives

Primary Aim

To architect an autonomous AI agent capable of multi-source reasoning, combining local documents with real-time database verification.

Core Objectives

  • Automation: 100% automated student & fee verification.
  • Live Tracking: Real-time scraping of UoS web announcements.
  • Zero Hallucination: Data-gated prompting for high integrity.
  • Scalability: Multi-platform deployment (Cloud/Local).
